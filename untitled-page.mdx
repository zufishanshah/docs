---

Define characteristics of jobs and positions in the job catalog before filling jobs or positions in a supervisory organization. A job catalog can include levels, titles, grades, compensation, and other attributes.

1. [**Create Job Profiles**](https://doc.workday.com/admin-guide/en-us/human-capital-management/staffing/job-catalog/dan1370797329403.html). This describes the jobs or positions in your organization. A job profile can belong to one or more job families. You can also configure workflows, such as review and approval, for creating and editing job profiles by setting up the _Manage Job Profile_ business process.
2. [**Create Job Families**](https://doc.workday.com/admin-guide/en-us/human-capital-management/staffing/job-catalog/dan1370796359541.html). This groups job profiles within the job catalog. A job family can belong to a job family group.
3. [**Create Job Family Groups**](https://doc.workday.com/admin-guide/en-us/human-capital-management/staffing/job-catalog/dan1370796359251.html). A job family group represents a collection of job families.
